# Harbor Protocol

Open liquidity for bonds, built on Uniswap v4.

::::steps

### Bond holders

Sell locked-token bonds instantly — no need to wait until the unlock date or navigate complex OTC deals. Lighthouse's Harbor AMM offers on-demand, transparent pricing powered by algorithmic market making.

### Capital Providers

Supply liquidity to earn native yield and trading fees. Deepen your exposure to assets you’re already holding for the long term, while contributing to ecosystem growth.

### Bond Issuers

Give recipients the flexibility to sell their bonds without negating the impact of keeping tokens locked until a future date.
